AICTE Pragati Scheme

Pragati Scholarship for Girls 2026

₹50,000/year National Level Deadline: 30 April 2026

About the Scholarship

The Pragati Scholarship is a Government of India scheme implemented by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) to provide financial assistance to promising young women pursuing technical education (Degree or Diploma courses).

This initiative aims to empower women through technical education, helping them to build successful careers and contribute to national development.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Gender: Must be a girl student.
  • Education Level: Must be admitted to the First year of a Degree/Diploma course in an AICTE-approved institution.
  • Family Limit: Up to two girls per family are eligible.
  • Income Limit: Family income from all sources must not exceed ₹8,000,000 per annum.

Benefits & Rewards

Selected students will receive the following financial support:

  • Amount: ₹50,000 per annum.
  • Coverage: The amount can be used for college fees, purchasing computers, stationeries, books, equipment, or software.
  • Duration: Maximum 4 years for Degree courses and 3 years for Diploma courses.
